Welcome to the UFABC ImmersiveLab

The UFABC ImmersiveLab, opened in 2025, is part of the Center for Mathematics, Computation, and Cognition (CMCC) at the Federal University of ABC (UFABC). The lab focuses on Cognitive Computer Vision, Virtual and Augmented Reality, 360° Video, and Cybersecurity, integrating students and researchers from Brazil and abroad.
