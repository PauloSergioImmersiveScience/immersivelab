About the Laboratory

The UFABC ImmersiveLab, established in 2025, is a multidisciplinary space dedicated to research in immersive technologies. Located at the Federal University of ABC (UFABC), the lab is affiliated with the Center for Mathematics, Computation, and Cognition (CMCC).

Our mission is to explore the frontiers of human-computer interaction through artificial intelligence, immersive environments, and computer vision. We develop innovative solutions in collaboration with partners such as:

  • National partners: LNCC (National Laboratory for Scientific Computing), USP University Hospital
  • International partners: University of Idaho (USA), University of Porto (Portugal)
